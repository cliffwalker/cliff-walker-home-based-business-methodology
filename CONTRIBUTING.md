# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in the **Cliff Walker Home-Based Business Methodology**.

This repository represents a governed reference framework. Contributions are welcome where appropriate, but must follow the principles and controls outlined below to preserve clarity, integrity, and long-term consistency.

---

## Purpose of Contributions

Contributions are intended to:

- Improve clarity, structure, or accuracy  
- Correct errors or omissions  
- Enhance documentation quality  
- Support maintainability and long-term use  
- Strengthen governance, ethics, or usability  

This repository is **not** open for unrestricted modification or reinterpretation of the methodology.

---

## Contribution Principles

All contributions must:

1. Align with the ethical and governance standards defined in `GOVERNANCE.md`
2. Preserve the intent and structure of the methodology
3. Avoid introducing misleading, promotional, or outcome-based claims
4. Maintain the “home-based business” framing
5. Respect responsible and transparent use of AI
6. Be clearly scoped and documented

---

## What Can Be Contributed

Appropriate contribution types include:

- Typographical or formatting corrections  
- Clarity improvements to existing text  
- Structural or navigation enhancements  
- Documentation refinements  
- Explanatory notes or definitions  
- Improvements to templates or layouts  
- Suggestions that strengthen governance or usability  

---

## What Should Not Be Submitted

The following should **not** be submitted without explicit approval:

- Claims of income, earnings, or guaranteed outcomes  
- Marketing or promotional language  
- Testimonials or personal success stories  
- Medical, financial, or legal advice  
- Material that alters the core intent of the methodology  
- Unauthorized rebranding or derivative positioning  
- Content that removes or weakens governance safeguards  

---

## How to Propose a Change

All contributions should follow this process:

1. Clearly identify the file or section affected  
2. Describe the proposed change  
3. Explain the reason for the change  
4. Describe the expected impact  
5. Confirm alignment with governance principles  

Submissions that do not include this context may be declined.

---

## Review & Approval

- All changes are subject to review before acceptance  
- The methodology maintainer reserves the right to approve, reject, or request revisions  
- Accepted changes will be recorded in `CHANGELOG.md`  
- Versioned releases will reflect significant updates  

---

## Attribution & Credit

Contributions may be acknowledged where appropriate, but authorship of the methodology remains with Cliff Walker.

Contributing does not imply ownership, endorsement, or authority over the framework.

---

## Respectful Collaboration

All contributors are expected to engage respectfully and professionally.

Harassment, misleading content, or attempts to bypass governance controls are not permitted.

---

## Questions or Clarifications

If you are unsure whether a change is appropriate, please seek clarification before submitting.

Thank you for helping maintain the quality, integrity, and long-term value of this methodology.
