# The Global Home Business Methodology (Framework Summary)

## Who It Is For
Professionals transitioning out of corporate dependency who want:
- structure,
- ethical boundaries,
- repeatable systems,
- disciplined progress without hype or income claims.

## Core Architecture
- Six Master Tasks (core work streams)
- staged progression (capability before complexity)
- daily operating cadence (Single Daily Action)
- prospecting and follow-up logging
- weekly governance review loop
- Responsible AI rules (productivity without truth drift)
